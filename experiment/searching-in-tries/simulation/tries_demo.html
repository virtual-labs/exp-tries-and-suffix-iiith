• Type in a word in the INSERT BOX and hit the INSERT BUTTON.
  • The word would be attached to the trie, step by step. Understand the steps well.
  • Repeat this, adding a few more words and observing the pattern.
  • Type in a word in the SEARCH BOX and hit the SEARCH BUTTON.
  • The word would be searched for, nodes being colored one by one. Observe the patten.
  • Hit reset searches to clear all colored nodes, and reset inserts to start afresh.
  • You can click on the tiny black button at the top-right corner of any card to collapse it.
